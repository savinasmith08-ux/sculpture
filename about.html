OUR STORY

Three Decades of Curating Excellence in Three Dimensions

A Legacy of Fine Art

Founded in 1990, Sculpturesite Gallery has spent over 35 years dedicated exclusively to the exhibition and promotion of contemporary fine art sculpture. We believe that sculpture, as a medium, offers a unique engagement with space, material, and the human experience.

Originally established in San Francisco and now located in the beautiful Sonoma Valley, we have curated thousands of works for private collectors, corporate headquarters, and healthcare institutions across the globe.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between the visionary artist and the discerning collector, ensuring that every piece finds a space where it is not just seen, but felt.
